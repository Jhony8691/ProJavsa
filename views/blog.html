3 Free Resources to Get Started in Project Management

In today's next section, we want to provide you with some free tips and resources to get you started in the field of project management

  • On many occasions professionals who want to start in management or who want to turn their career around write to us, however, the available economic resources can be a limitation, but it does not have to be.
  • Today we have many free resources that can help beginners get started on this path in Project Management.
  • Let's go with that:

3 Free project management guides

  • The European Commission makes available a complete and detailed project management guide, describing the PM2 methodology.
  • Available in English and Spanish, this guide is completely free and has detailed information on how to manage a project from start to finish.
  • It also includes 33 professional templates, which will help you put into practice what you have learned and are also free to use.
  • You can access your download here: PM2 Guide
  • You also have at your disposal the official Scrum guide for free, in case you are interested in this highly demanded Agile framework.
  • You can access your download here: Scrum Guide

PMI Kickoff

  • This is a free course of approximately 1 hour duration, created and distributed free of charge by PMI.
  • It is an introductory training to project management, and to the different approaches (traditional, agile, hybrid). highly recommended and free
  • You can access the course here: PMI Kickoff
